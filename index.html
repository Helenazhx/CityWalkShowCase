Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the video download so large?
To deliver the most immersive and high-quality experience possible on Apple Vision Pro, we use 3D stereoscopic video at 4K resolution. This ensures incredible visual fidelity but requires significant data. We've optimized the videos as much as possible without compromising quality.
Can I use the app offline after downloading?
Yes! Once you've downloaded the video content, you can enjoy the immersive experience without an internet connection, making it perfect for travel or situations with limited connectivity.
