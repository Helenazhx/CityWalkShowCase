Why is the video download so large?
To deliver the most immersive and high-quality experience possible on Apple Vision Pro, we use 3D stereoscopic video at 8K resolution. This ensures incredible visual fidelity but requires significant data. We've optimized the videos as much as possible without compromising quality.
Will the downloaded content take up permanent space on my device?
The app uses Apple's On-Demand Resources system, which means the system can automatically remove the downloaded content if you're low on storage and haven't used the app recently. When you open the app again, it will re-download as needed.
Can I use the app offline after downloading?
Yes! Once you've downloaded the video content, you can enjoy the immersive experience without an internet connection, making it perfect for travel or situations with limited connectivity.
How do I switch between different locations in Shanghai?
From the main menu, you can browse different experiences using the carousel interface. Simply swipe or tap to navigate between locations and tap to select the one you want to experience.
Does the app support both English and Chinese?
Yes, Shanghai City Walk fully supports both English and Chinese languages. The app will automatically use your device's language setting, or you can manually switch languages in the app settings.
Will you add more locations in future updates?
Absolutely! We're already filming additional locations throughout Shanghai and plan to release regular updates with new immersive experiences at no additional cost.
